WOW - WellBeing Of Waste


Our company (Getster Tech Private Limited) is a product-based organization focused on recycling used notebooks and waste collections. We've partnered with Venkraft Papers, a renowned industry player, to collect used notebooks from schools, colleges, and other educational institutions. In exchange, we provide them with tailored educational applications. Among our flagship programs is WOW - WellBeing Of Waste, where I contribute major applications such as Manage WOW Flashcards, Holidays - Non Operational Days, Wow Local Staff Management, WOW Screens, Link WOW Subjects, and others. Our overarching project, WOW - Wellbeing of Waste, aims to promote sustainability by recycling waste materials.
